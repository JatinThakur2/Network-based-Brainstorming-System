About Portal

Brainstorming, and Multi-voting are approaches for generating, categorizing, and choosing among ideas from a group of people. Using these techniques encourages every person within the group to contribute, instead of just one or two. They spark creativity in group members as they listen to the ideas of others and generate a substantial list ofideas, rather than just the few things that first come to mind. Finally, the techniques allow a group of people to choose among ideas or options thoughtfully.
